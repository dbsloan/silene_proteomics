# silene_proteomics
Code and data for analysis of LC-MS/MS data from purified mitochondria and chloroplasts from Arabidopsis thaliana, Agrostemma githago, and Silene conica


### Directories
- [Protocols](Protocols): Lab protocols for purification of mitochondrial and chloroplast fractions
- [Quantification](Quantification): LC-MS/MS quantification data by PSMs and peak abundance
- [Organellar_proteins](Organellar_proteins): Analysis of enrichment of mitochondrial-encoded and plastid-encoded proteins in organellar fractions
- [aaRS_enrichment](aaRS_enrichment): Analysis of enrichment of aaRS proteins and plastid-encoded proteins in organellar fractions
- [PheRS](PheRS): Structural modeling and analysis of PheRS enrichment
- [Mitoribosome](Mitoribosome): Structural modeling of mitoribosome subunits
